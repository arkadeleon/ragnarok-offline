# Copy Ragnarok Online Client Files to Your Device

## Method 1: Wi-Fi (Windows to iPhone/iPad)

**Step 1 — Share the folder on Windows**

1. Right-click the kRO client folder and select **Properties**
2. Open the **Sharing** tab and click **Advanced Sharing**
3. Check **Share this folder** and click **OK**
4. Open **Settings** → **Network & Internet** and verify your network profile is **Private**
5. In **Control Panel** → **Network and Sharing Center** → **Change advanced sharing settings**, enable **Network discovery** and **File and printer sharing**

**Step 2 — Find your PC's IP address**

Press **Win + R**, type `cmd`, press Enter, then type `ipconfig`. Note the **IPv4 Address** (e.g. `192.168.1.10`).

**Step 3 — Connect from iOS**

1. Open the **Files** app
2. Tap **···** in the top-right corner and select **Connect to Server**
3. Enter `smb://192.168.1.10` (use your PC's IP address)
4. Enter your Windows username and password, then tap **Connect**

**Step 4 — Copy your files**

Copy the following to **On My iPhone/iPad → Ragnarok Offline** (long-press each item → **Copy**, navigate to destination → **Paste**):

- **data.grf**
- **BGM** folder

---

## Method 2: USB Cable

**Mac:** Connect your device → open **Finder** → select your iPhone/iPad → **Files** tab → expand **Ragnarok Offline** → drag in your files.

**Windows:** Connect your device → open **iTunes** → select your device → **File Sharing** → select **Ragnarok Offline** → drag in your files.
